At present I am part of the Institute for Risk and Uncertainty at the University of Liverpool. My current work focuses on uncertainty quantification for engineering (and not only) computer models through the DATA-CENTRIC project (with Dr Alex Diaz ). More info in Projects. My research interests are mainly in the areas of computational engineering for aircraft design, uncertainty quantification, aerodynamics and machine learning (roughly in this order). I am particularly interested in the principled approach to uncertainty quantification for the design of non-standard and innovative aerospace solutions.

Academic Background

I hold a PhD degree in computational engineering and uncertainty quantification from the University of Liverpool. I got my Bachelor degree in aerospace engineering at the same university.
